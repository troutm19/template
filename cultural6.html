Anticipated Effects

Creating more programs with a tech. free objective will help lessen the technology craving. People can learn that the world doesn’t revolve around their phones and realize it’s possible to have fun without the constant burden of technology. Taking part in these programs or even being aware of their existence can help motivate individuals to limit their technology usage. Getting information out about the benefits of being active versus being glued to an electronic can open individuals’ eyes to the harsh reality that technology could be taking us away from a healthy future.

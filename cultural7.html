Conclusion

Overall, programs that push people to turn off the electronics and join in on other activities are the best way to combat the technology addiction people experience today. With these programs the constant craving to hold a phone or check social media will be replaced by healthier tech. free alternatives. Movies like Wall-E that accurately depict the future dangers that society can succumb to in the future, should be a wake up call to all tech-crazed individuals. Wall-E gives great examples of how human’s dependency on technology, if continued, can end badly in the future. Human’s dependency on technology will only escalate in future years; this reliance, if continued, can have many negative consequences. The animated Pixar film illustrates these consequences through the depiction of society’s unhealthy lifestyle. In order to prevent a bleak outcome like the one shown in Wall-E, programs that encourage losing the technology for more productive activities should be created.
